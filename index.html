    Flipkart Bravo Award Case Study

    Crisis Management during Flood Event

    During monsoon season, flash floods heavily disrupted access to the 130,000 sq ft Flipkart fulfillment center. Major logistics corridors were blocked and a substantial number of operational crew members could not reach the shifts, creating a critical labor shortage.

    The Challenge: Maintain uninterrupted inbound and outbound dispatches of 200,000+ daily units, avoid sorting backlogs, and prevent severe SLA breaches on high-priority shipments.

    Instant Cross-Training & Reallocation Rapidly cross-trained the available 30% workforce on sortation and outbound packing tasks to cover critical bottlenecks.
    Vendor & 3PL Integration Coordinated with active transport partners and security teams to redirect shipment dispatches through secondary dry routes.
    Zero SLA Breaches Cleared the returns queue and completed outbound targets with no failures, securing business continuity.

    Yashika's hands-on leadership, shift-wise performance tracking, and strategic deployment of emergency personnel ensured zero major failures and won her the prestigious Bravo Award from Flipkart management.
